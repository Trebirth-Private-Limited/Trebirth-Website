What We Do

Revolutionizing agriculture through innovative pest detection and sustainable farming solutions

Early Pest Detection

We specialize in detecting stem borers and termite before visible symptoms appear, using advanced sensing technology and machine learning algorithms to identify threats at their earliest stages.

Sustainable Solutions

Our eco-friendly approach minimizes chemical pesticide usage by providing targeted treatment recommendations, promoting biodiversity and environmental health while maintaining crop productivity.

Yield Optimization

Through continuous monitoring and data analytics, we help farmers maximize their crop yields by preventing pest damage and optimizing agricultural practices for sustainable growth.

Our Advanced Process

1

Visual Screening & Identification

Using advanced imaging techniques, we perform initial screening to narrow down and identify potentially infected areas from healthy ones.

  • High-resolution imaging to capture visual cues
  • Detection of stress indicators in plants and structures
  • Identification of subtle changes in appearance
  • AI-powered analysis of visual symptoms
2

Precise Location Detection

Combined scanning using our advanced radar devices and sensor technology to pinpoint the exact location of pest infestations.

  • Non-invasive scanning for internal analysis
  • Advanced sensor technology for detailed pest location mapping
  • Visualization of pest tunnels and damage patterns
  • Precise coordinate identification for targeted action
3

Targeted Solutions

Based on the precise location data, we offer specialized solutions to address pest problems effectively.

Stem Borer Treatment

Precision techniques to eliminate borers at their exact location, ensuring minimal damage to healthy plant tissue.

Termite Detection Assistance

4

Continuous Monitoring & Assessment

  • Real-time effectiveness tracking
  • Health recovery monitoring
  • Preventive measures implementation
  • Detailed reporting and recommendations
