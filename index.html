About Trebirth PVT. LTD.

Trebirth Private Limited is an innovative agri-tech startup dedicated to solving the critical problem of early stem borer detection in crops. Our mission is to help farmers identify infestations before visible symptoms appear, enabling timely intervention to protect plant life and productivity.

We bring together a passionate team of hardware engineers, software developers, and agricultural experts to create targeted solutions for the agriculture industry. Through our collaborations with domain specialists and research institutions, we've developed cutting-edge technology that addresses one of farming's most persistent challenges.

Our approach combines advanced sensing technology with machine learning algorithms to detect stem borers at the earliest possible stage, giving farmers the information they need to take action before significant damage occurs.

Our Technology

Innovative solutions designed to protect your crops at every stage

Smart Tagging

Our imaging solution analyzes leaf patterns to identify early-stage infections, helping narrow down suspicious trees for targeted intervention.

Early Detection

Our handheld detection device analyzes each plant during manual scanning to identify subtle signs of stem borer infestation, ensuring quick and accurate response.

Targeted Treatment

Our system provides precise treatment recommendations, reducing pesticide use while maximizing effectiveness against stem borers.

Data Analytics

Comprehensive reporting and analytics help you understand infestation patterns and optimize your farming practices.
