How It Works

Our innovative four-step process for detecting stem borer infestations

1

Placement & Scanning

Position the handheld device near vulnerable crops and align it with the stem. The built-in sensors perform precise scans, capturing variations associated with borer activity. Each reading provides accurate, real-time feedback, helping users assess infestation levels directly in the field.

2

AI Analysis

The collected data is processed through proprietary algorithms that analyze signal variations in real time. These algorithms identify distinctive patterns linked to stem borer activity. The results provide clear, actionable insights for early and accurate detection

3

Alert & Reporting

As the device scans, live data is transmitted to the mobile app for instant visualization. Users can interpret the results easily and follow guided recommendations for timely intervention.
